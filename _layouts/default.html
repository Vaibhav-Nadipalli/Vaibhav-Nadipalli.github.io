Biography

I'm a secondary school student obsessed with technology, self-improvement, and business. Here, I'll share some of my projects and occasional blog posts.

My journey started with binge-watching MKBHD videos, which sparked my interest in tech. This led me to discover how fascinating physics can be. The real turning point was reading "Sapiens" by Yuval Noah Harari. This book gave me a deeper understanding of the human experience and inspired me to make a positive impact.

I realized that the best way to achieve this is through business, and now my goal is to become a tech startup founder.
